VEGITARIN RECIPES

Indian Cuisine is well known for its large collection of Vegetarian dishes. This collection of Indian Vegetarian Recipes will help you make everything you need! Right from appetizers, curries, rice dishes and dinner ideas. These vegetarian recipes are simple, delicious and can easily be cooked by newbies.
Most of the dishes are made using vegetables, grains, paneer and lentils. A lot of these are vegan or vegan-friendly with substitutes provided in the recipes.

  1. Chana Masala
  2. Nuts, nut butters, and seeds
  3. Hemp, flax, and chia seeds
  4. Tofu and other minimally processed meat substitutes
  5. Calcium-fortified plant milks and yogurts
  6. Seaweed
